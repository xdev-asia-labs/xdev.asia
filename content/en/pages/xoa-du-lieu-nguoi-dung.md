## Delete user data

At xDev Asia, we respect your privacy and control over your personal data. This page provides instructions on how to request deletion of your account and all related data.

### Data we store

When you use xDev Asia (website and mobile application), we may store:

- **Account information:** Display name, email address, avatar
- **Learning history:** Lessons completed, course progress
- **Bookmark data:** Saved articles and series
- **Quiz results:** Scores and practice test history

### How to request data deletion

You can request deletion of all personal data in one of the following ways:

**Method 1: Via email**

Send an email to **duy@xdev.asia** with subject line **"Account deletion request"** and content including:

- Registered email address
- Name displayed on account
- Confirm you want to delete all data

**Method 2: Via GitHub**

Create an issue at [github.com/xdev-asia-labs](https://github.com/xdev-asia-labs) with the title **"Data Deletion Request"**.

### Processing time

We will process the request within **30 days** of receipt. Once completed, you will receive an email notification confirming the data has been deleted.

### Data is deleted

Once the request is approved, we will delete:

- Account and personal information
- Study history and progress
- Bookmarks and custom data
- Quiz results and scores

**Note:** Some anonymized and aggregated data (e.g. view statistics) may not be deleted because they cannot be linked back to you.

### Contact

If you have questions about privacy or data processing, please contact:

- **Email:** duy@xdev.asia
- **Privacy policy:** [xdev.asia/pages/chinh-sach-quyen-rieng-tu/](/pages/chinh-sach-quyen-rieng-tu/)
