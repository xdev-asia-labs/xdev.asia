## About xDev

xDev is a platform for sharing real-world technological knowledge, built by **Duy Tran** - Solution Architect with more than 10 years of experience designing and implementing enterprise systems in the fields of healthcare and finance.

The content at xDev is not textbook theory. Every article, series, and lesson comes from practical experience on large projects — from national vaccination systems, health applications serving millions of people, to enterprise-scale digital finance platforms.
