# Arrow Escape Puzzle Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** May 22, 2026

Arrow Escape Puzzle is a casual puzzle game published by xDev. This policy explains what information is used when you play the game.

## Information We Collect

Arrow Escape Puzzle does not require an account and does not ask you to provide a name, email address, password, contact list, photos, microphone access, camera access, or precise location.

The game stores gameplay progress, settings, language choice, coins, boosters, tutorial state, and purchase state locally on your device.

## Advertising and Purchases

The game may show ads through Google AdMob. AdMob and its partners may process device identifiers, approximate location, ad interactions, diagnostics, and app activity to provide, measure, and improve ads.

Optional in-app purchases are processed by Apple App Store or Google Play. xDev does not receive or store your full payment card details.

## How Information Is Used

Information is used to provide gameplay, save progress, remember settings, show optional ads, verify purchases, prevent abuse, and improve stability.

## Children

Arrow Escape Puzzle is designed for a general audience. It is not directed to children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us and we will review the request.

## Third-Party Services

The game may use these third-party services:

- Google AdMob
- Apple App Store purchases
- Google Play Billing

Each third-party service processes information according to its own privacy policy.

## Data Deletion

You can delete local gameplay data by uninstalling the app from your device. For privacy questions or deletion requests related to support communications, contact us.
