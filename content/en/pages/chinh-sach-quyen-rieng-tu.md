We collect the information you provide when registering: full name, email, password (encrypted). Additionally, we collect usage data such as learning progress and platform activity.

- Send course notifications and important updates
- Analyze and improve the platform
### 3. Data security
We use industry standard security measures to protect your personal information, including SSL encryption and secure storage.
### 4. Share information
We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties, unless required by law.
### 5. Cookies
We use cookies to maintain login sessions and improve user experience. You can disable cookies in your browser.
