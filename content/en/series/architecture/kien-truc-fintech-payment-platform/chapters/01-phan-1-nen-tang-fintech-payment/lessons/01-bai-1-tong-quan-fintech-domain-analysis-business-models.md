## Introduction

FinTech (Financial Technology) has completely changed the way we interact with financial services. From paying by QR code at a coffee shop to borrowing money online in 5 minutes — all are operated by behind-the-scenes FinTech systems.

In this first lesson, we will comprehensively analyze the FinTech domain, understanding the main verticals, business models, and regulatory landscape — especially in the Vietnamese market.

---

## 1. What is FinTech?

### 1.1 Definition

**FinTech** is the combination of **Finance** and **Technology** — using technology to improve, automate, and disrupt traditional financial services.

```
FinTech = Financial Services + Modern Technology
        = Traditional Banking Problems + Software Solutions
```

### 1.2 Evolution of FinTech

| Phase | Period | Features |
|-----------|---------|-----------|
| **FinTech 1.0** | 1866-1967 | Telegraph, transatlantic cables, Telex |
| **FinTech 2.0** | 1967-2008 | ATMs, SWIFT, online banking |
| **FinTech 3.0** | 2008-2020 | Mobile payments, P2P lending, neobanks |
| **FinTech 4.0** | 2020-present | Embedded finance, DeFi, AI-powered, super apps |

### 1.3 Why is FinTech important for Software Engineers?

- **Huge market**: Global FinTech market reaches $340B by 2026
- **Vietnam market**: 50+ million e-wallet users, 80% of the population has smartphones
- **High salary**: FinTech engineers have top salaries in the market
- **Technical challenges**: High-throughput, consistency, security, compliance

## 2. Main Verticals in FinTech

### 2.1 Payments — Payments

Largest and most popular Vertical:

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│              PAYMENTS ECOSYSTEM              │
├─────────────┬──────────────┬────────────────┤
│   Online    │   In-store   │    P2P/P2M     │
│  Payments   │  (POS/QR)    │   Transfer     │
├─────────────┼──────────────┼────────────────┤
│ Payment     │ Merchant     │ Cross-border   │
│ Gateway     │ Acquiring    │ Remittance     │
├─────────────┼──────────────┼────────────────┤
│ E-wallet    │ Buy Now      │ Subscription   │
│             │ Pay Later    │ Billing        │
└─────────────┴──────────────┴────────────────┘
```

**Examples in Vietnam**: VNPay, MoMo, ZaloPay, ShopeePay, VietQR

### 2.2 Lending — Lending

```
Consumer Lending          Business Lending
├── Personal loans        ├── SME loans
├── Buy Now Pay Later     ├── Invoice financing
├── Microloans            ├── Supply chain finance
└── Student loans         └── Revenue-based financing
```

**Example**: MoMo (consumer loans), Tima, Fiin Credit

### 2.3 Digital Banking — Digital banking

- **Neobanks**: Completely digital bank (no branches)
- **Challenger banks**: Traditional bank + digital transformation
- **Banking-as-a-Service (BaaS)**: API-first banking infrastructure

**Examples in Vietnam**: Timo, TNEX, Cake by VPBank

### 2.4 InsurTech — Technology insurance

- On-demand insurance, parametric insurance
- Claims automation with AI
- Telematics-based pricing

### 2.5 WealthTech & Investment

- Robo-advisors, fractional investing
- Social trading, copy trading
- Crypto exchanges and DeFi

## 3. Business Models in FinTech

### 3.1 Revenue Models

| Model | Description | Example |
|-------|--------|-------|
| **Transaction fees** | % or fixed fee per transaction | Stripe (2.9% + $0.30) |
| **Interchange fee** | Fee from network card | Visa, Mastercard |
| **Subscription** | Monthly/annual fee | Banking apps premium |
| **Interest spread** | Interest rate differential | Lending platforms |
| **FX markup** | Spread on exchange rate | Wise, cross-border |
| **Freemium** | Free basic + paid premium | MoMo, ZaloPay |
| **Data monetization** | Analytics, insights | Credit scoring data |

## 5. Regulatory Landscape

### 5.1 Regulations in Vietnam

| Regulations | Content | Applies to |
|----------|-------|-------------|
| **Decree 101/2012** | Non-cash payment | Payment intermediaries |
| **Circular 39/2014** | Activities providing payment intermediary services | E-wallets, payment gateways |
| **Decree 35/2023** | Amendments to non-cash payments | All payment services |
| **Electronic Transaction Law 2023** | Digital signatures, electronic contracts | Digital contracts |
| **FinTech Sandbox** | Regulatory sandbox for FinTech | New FinTech products |

### 5.2 International regulations

- **PCI-DSS**: Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard
- **PSD2/PSD3**: Payment Services Directive (EU)
- **SOX**: Sarbanes-Oxley Act (financial reporting)
- **GDPR/PDPA**: Data protection regulations
- **Basel III**: Banking capital requirements

## 7. Technical Challenges in FinTech

### 7.1 Key challenges

| Challenge | Description | Solution |
|-----------|--------|-----------|
| **Consistency** | Don't lose money | ACID, Saga, Idempotency |
| **Availability** | 99.99%+ uptime | Multi-region, failover |
| **Throughput** | Millions TPS peak | Horizontal scaling |
| **Latency** | < 100ms response | Caching, async processing |
| **Security** | PCI-DSS, encryption | HSM, tokenization, WAF |
| **Compliance** | Regulatory reporting | Audit trail, immutable logs |
| **Reconciliation** | Exact matching | Double-entry ledger |

### 7.2 FinTech System Characteristics

```
Financial System Requirements:
├── Correctness > Performance (money can't be lost)
├── Auditability (every transaction must be traceable)
├── Idempotency (duplicate requests must be safe)
├── Consistency (balances must always be correct)
├── Security (defense in depth)
└── Compliance (regulatory requirements must be met)
```
