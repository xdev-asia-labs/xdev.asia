## Introduction

The **AWS Certified AI Practitioner (AIF-C01) Exam Prep** course helps you study systematically, covering all 5 domains of the exam — from AI/ML fundamentals to GenAI, Amazon Bedrock, Responsible AI, and Security.

### Who Should Take This Course?

- Developers, DevOps engineers, Solution Architects seeking an AI certification
- Business Analysts, Product Managers wanting to understand AI on AWS
- Beginners in AI who want to build a solid foundation
- Those with basic AI knowledge looking to validate it with an AWS certification

### AIF-C01 Exam Structure

| Domain | Weight | Lessons |
|--------|--------|---------|
| Domain 1: Fundamentals of AI and ML | 20% | Lessons 1–2 |
| Domain 2: Fundamentals of Generative AI | 24% | Lessons 3–4 |
| Domain 3: Applications of Foundation Models | 28% | Lessons 5–8 |
| Domain 4: Guidelines for Responsible AI | 14% | Lessons 9–10 |
| Domain 5: Security, Compliance & Governance | 14% | Lessons 11–12 |

- **Questions**: 65 scored + 15 unscored = 80 total questions
- **Duration**: 90 minutes
- **Passing score**: 700/1000
- **Exam fee**: $100 USD
- **Languages**: English (and other languages available)
- **Format**: Pearson VUE testing center or online proctored
