Your Privacy Choices**Last updated:** March 3, 2026At xDev, we respect your privacy and give you control over your personal data. This page explains how you can manage your privacy preferences and exercise your data rights.## 1. Manage Your Account DataYou can manage your personal information directly from your account settings:
- 
**Profile Information:** Update or remove your name, avatar, and bio from your Profile Settings

- **Email Preferences:** Manage email notification preferences in your account settings
- **Account Deletion:** You may request complete deletion of your account and all associated data by contacting us
## 2. Cookie PreferencesWe use the following types of cookies:### Essential Cookies (Required)These cookies are necessary for the Service to function and cannot be turned off. They include session cookies and CSRF protection tokens.### Analytics Cookies (Optional)We use analytics cookies (such as Google Analytics) to understand how visitors interact with our Service. You can opt out of analytics tracking:
- Use your browser's "Do Not Track" setting
- 
Install the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on

- Disable cookies in your browser settings
## 3. Communication PreferencesYou can control communications from xDev:- **Marketing Emails:** Unsubscribe using the link at the bottom of any marketing email
- **Course Notifications:** Manage in your notification settings
- **Newsletter:** Unsubscribe at any time via the unsubscribe link*Note: We may still send essential transactional emails (account security, payment receipts).*## 4. Data Access and PortabilityYou have the right to:- **Access your data:** Request a complete copy of all personal data we hold about you
- **Export your data:** Download your course progress, certificates, and profile information
- **Transfer your data:** Receive your data in a machine-readable format## 5. Right to Deletion ("Right to be Forgotten")You can request the deletion of your personal data. Upon request, we will:- Delete your account and profile information
- Remove your course progress and quiz data
- Delete your comments and reviews
- Remove you from all mailing lists*Note: Some data may be retained for legal compliance (e.g., payment records for tax purposes) for the legally required period.*## 6. Do Not Track (DNT)We honor "Do Not Track" browser signals. When we detect a DNT signal, we disable non-essential analytics tracking for that session.## 7. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):- **Right to Know:** What personal information we collect, use, and disclose
- **Right to Delete:** Request deletion of your personal information
- **Right to Opt-Out:** Opt out of the "sale" of personal information (we do not sell your data)
- **Right to Non-Discrimination:** We will not discriminate against you for exercising your privacy rights## 8. European Privacy Rights (GDPR)If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):- Right to access, rectification, and erasure
- Right to restrict or object to processing
- Right to data portability
- Right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority## 9. How to Exercise Your RightsTo exercise any of the privacy rights described above, you can:
- 
**Email us:** duy@xdev.asia

- **Use in-app settings:** Manage preferences directly from your account dashboard
We will respond to your request within 30 days. We may need to verify your identity before processing your request.## 10. Contact InformationFor any privacy-related questions or concerns:
