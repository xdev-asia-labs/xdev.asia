# Chinh sach quyen rieng tu Arrow Escape Puzzle

**Cap nhat lan cuoi:** 22/05/2026

Arrow Escape Puzzle la game giai do thong thuong do xDev phat hanh. Chinh sach nay giai thich thong tin nao duoc su dung khi ban choi game.

## Thong tin chung toi thu thap

Arrow Escape Puzzle khong yeu cau tai khoan va khong yeu cau ban cung cap ten, email, mat khau, danh ba, anh, microphone, camera hoac vi tri chinh xac.

Game luu tien trinh choi, cai dat, ngon ngu, coin, booster, trang thai huong dan va trang thai mua hang tren thiet bi cua ban.

## Quang cao va mua hang

Game co the hien thi quang cao thong qua Google AdMob. AdMob va cac doi tac co the xu ly ma dinh danh thiet bi, vi tri gan dung, tuong tac voi quang cao, chan doan loi va hoat dong trong ung dung de cung cap, do luong va cai thien quang cao.

Mua hang trong ung dung la tuy chon va duoc xu ly boi Apple App Store hoac Google Play. xDev khong nhan hoac luu thong tin the thanh toan day du cua ban.

## Cach su dung thong tin

Thong tin duoc su dung de cung cap gameplay, luu tien trinh, ghi nho cai dat, hien thi quang cao tuy chon, xac minh mua hang, ngan chan lam dung va cai thien do on dinh.

## Tre em

Arrow Escape Puzzle duoc thiet ke cho doi tuong pho thong va khong huong den tre em duoi 13 tuoi. Neu ban cho rang tre em da cung cap thong tin ca nhan, vui long lien he de chung toi xem xet yeu cau.

## Dich vu ben thu ba

Game co the su dung cac dich vu ben thu ba sau:

- Google AdMob
- Apple App Store purchases
- Google Play Billing

Moi dich vu ben thu ba xu ly thong tin theo chinh sach quyen rieng tu rieng cua ho.
